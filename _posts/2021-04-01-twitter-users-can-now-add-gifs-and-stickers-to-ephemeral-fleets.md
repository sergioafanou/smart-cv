Twitter today added options to include stickers and other media in its ephemeral tweets, or "Fleets," which last for 24 hours on the social network before disappearing. 
<br/>

<br/>
<img src="https://images.macrumors.com/article-new/2021/04/twitter-fleets.jpg" alt="" width="920" height="613" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-791916" />
<br/>
Launched on the platform <a href="https://www.macrumors.com/2020/11/17/twitter-launches-fleets-ephemeral-tweets/">back in November</a>, Fleets are short stories made up of photos or videos with overlaying text. Much like stories on Instagram or Facebook, Fleets are located at the top of the Twitter timeline.
<br/>

<br/>
Now when Twitter users compose a Fleet, they'll see a smiley face icon at the bottom of the screen for adding animated stickers and emoji ("Twemoji) to their creation. Meanwhile, searching for something in the search bar at the top of the screen pulls up GIFs from Tenor and Giphy.

<h2>How to Create a Fleet on Twitter</h2>
<br/>
<ol><li>Tap your <strong>profile image</strong> in the Twitter app.</li>
<br/>
<li>Tap the <strong>Fleet compose</strong> icon.</li>
<br/>
<li>Compose your message, using text, emoji, photos, a GIF, or video. To add media, tap the screen to bring up the keyboard and toolbar. There, you'll find the <strong>Align</strong> button to format text, a <strong>B</strong> button to bold text, and a <strong>Highlight</strong> button to highlight it. The color wheel can be used to change the text and highlight color, and the circle icon at the bottom left of the screen to changes the background color.
<br/>
<img src="https://images.macrumors.com/article-new/2021/04/how-to-fleet-twitter-e1617279985599.jpg" alt="twitter" width="5133" height="2760" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-791921" />
<br/>
<li>Tap the <strong>Fleet</strong> button to post the Fleet to your profile, where it'll remain for 24 hours.</li></ol>To delete a posted Fleet, simply select it and tap <strong>Delete Fleet</strong>.
